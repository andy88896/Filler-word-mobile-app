# Privacy Policy for Filler Word Coach

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

## Introduction

Filler Word Coach ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application ("App") handles your information.

## Information Collection and Use

### What We Collect

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The App uses the following device features:
- **Microphone Access**: Used solely for real-time speech recognition on your device
- **Speech Recognition**: Processes your voice locally on your device using Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer API

### How We Use Your Information

- **Speech Processing**: Your voice is processed in real-time on your device to detect filler words (e.g., "um", "uh", "like")
- **Haptic Feedback**: When a filler word is detected, the App triggers a vibration on your device
- **Local Processing Only**: All speech recognition happens locally on your iPhone using iOS's built-in speech recognition. No audio or transcripts are sent to our servers or any third party

### What We DO NOT Do

- We do NOT record or save your voice or audio
- We do NOT store speech transcripts
- We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT share any information with third parties
- We do NOT sell any data

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's native iOS Speech Recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer). Apple's use of speech data is governed by their own privacy policy. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details on how Apple handles speech recognition data.

## Data Storage

The App does NOT store any user data. When you close the App or end a recording session, no data is retained.

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed for general audiences and can be used by anyone who wishes to improve their speaking habits.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Microphone Permission**: Required to listen for filler words during speech practice
- **Speech Recognition Permission**: Required to convert speech to text for filler word detection

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your iPhone's Settings > Privacy & Security.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete.
